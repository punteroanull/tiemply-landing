## Frequently Asked Questions

### Can I change plans at any time?
Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan whenever you want. Changes take effect immediately and costs are prorated.

### Is there a free trial?
Yes, all paid plans include a **14-day free trial** with no commitment.

### What payment methods do you accept?
We accept credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express) through Stripe.

### Can I cancel at any time?
Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time. You'll maintain access until the end of your billing period.

### What happens to my data if I cancel?
Your data is kept for 30 days after cancellation. You can export it or reactivate your account during this period.
